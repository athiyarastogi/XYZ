How QuiCloset Works

QuiCloset is the easy way to find the clothing you are looking for in the stores closest to you.


Browse



Search the item you are looking for and browse through different options in the stores closest to you. 


Check Store Availability


Reserve the item you like and the retailer will confirm in-store availability. 


Buy



Upon confirmation you can either walk in the store, try and buy the item or
request an instant pickup upon payment through the app.
